New Keyboard

Jun 13, 2021

I got around to building this new keyboard. It's essentially the lightcycle version of the Dactyl keyboard. The few differences are that the space and port for the board is bigger to accommodate a nice!nano board with pins.

The keyboard is wireless thanks to the nice!nano. The board has a USB-C connector, but I added these magnetic charging and data cables to make charging and connecting easy and lessen the force on the port on the board.

I can also sling the keyboard halves with some cord and wear it as a stylish scarf.
